WAKAHISA Hibiki / 若久 響 (SALADOFSTONES)
Background: Complicated Community

A proud, fiercely independent girl who's been around the magical block once or twice. She finds her non-magical life rather constricting, and so looks to her powers, and especially her position as Conservator, as a source of meaning in life. Hibiki lost her Conservatorship for some time before the beginning of the campaign, after being forced to take the fall for a botched mission — it was either falsely admitting guilt and getting to retain her magical abilities, or sticking to her guns and losing them. Only recently was she reinstated. She generally plays her cards quite close to her chest, but occasionally her affinity for detective stories and Soviet history leaks out, much to her chagrin.
